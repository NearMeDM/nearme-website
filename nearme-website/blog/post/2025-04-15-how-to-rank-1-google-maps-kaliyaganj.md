## Step 1 — Claim and Verify Your Google Business Profile

First, go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) and search for your business name.

**If your business is already listed:** Click "Claim this business" and go through verification.

**If it is not listed:** Click "Add your business" and fill in all the details.

Verification is done by **postcard** (Google sends a card with a code to your address) or by **phone/email** for eligible businesses. This takes 3–14 days.

> ⚠️ **Important:** Many Kaliyaganj businesses are already listed on Google by customers — but unclaimed. An unclaimed listing has wrong information and no owner control. Claim yours today.

## Step 2 — Fill Every Single Field Completely

Google rewards complete profiles. Most businesses fill in 40% of the available fields and wonder why they are not ranking.

Here is what you must fill in completely:

- **Business name** — exactly as it appears on your signboard
- **Category** — choose the most specific primary category, then add 2–3 secondary categories
- **Address** — precise address with landmark if possible
- **Phone number** — your WhatsApp-enabled number
- **Website** — even a simple one helps significantly
- **Hours** — include special hours for holidays and festivals
- **Description** — 750 characters, include your services and location keywords naturally
- **Attributes** — delivery, dine-in, wheelchair accessible etc.
- **Products/Services** — add every service with name, description and price

## Step 3 — Upload 20+ High-Quality Photos

Businesses with **10+ photos** get 35% more website clicks and 42% more direction requests, according to Google's own data.

Upload:
- Exterior photos (from street, easy to identify)
- Interior photos (clean, well-lit)
- Product photos (every product or dish you offer)
- Team photos (builds trust instantly)
- Process photos (how you work)

Name your photo files with keywords before uploading. Example: `dental-clinic-kaliyaganj-interior.jpg` instead of `IMG_0234.jpg`.

## Step 4 — Get More 5-Star Reviews (This is the Biggest Factor)

Google's algorithm weighs **review quantity and quality** more heavily than almost anything else for local rankings.

**How to get more reviews fast:**

1. After every sale, send the customer a WhatsApp message: *"Thank you for visiting! Could you spare 2 minutes to leave us a Google review? It helps us a lot: [your Google review link]"*
2. Put a small card on your counter with a QR code linking to your review page
3. Ask your top 10 loyal customers personally — most will happily do it

**Your Google Review link:** Go to your Google Business Profile → Get more reviews → Copy the short link.

## Step 5 — Post Weekly Google Business Updates

Most business owners do not know that Google Business Profile has a **Posts** feature — like a mini social media feed that appears directly in Google Search.

Posting once a week:
- Keeps your profile "active" in Google's eyes
- Gives customers fresh information (offers, events, news)
- Increases your click-through rate from search results

Post ideas:
- Weekly special offer
- New product or service launch
- Festival greeting with your business photo
- Before/after case study
- Customer success story

## Step 6 — Use the Right Keywords in Your Profile

Google reads every word in your profile to decide which searches to show you for.

Include these types of keywords naturally in your description and posts:
- Your service + city: *"dental clinic in Kaliyaganj"*
- Your service + "near me": *"best coaching centre near me"*
- Your service + district: *"grocery shop North Dinajpur"*
- Your service alone: *"ladies tailoring"*, *"mobile repair"*

**Do not** stuff keywords unnaturally. Write for humans first, Google second.
